# COOKIE POLICY### SiteHub Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2025##### **What is a cookie?**

A cookie is a small text file stored on your device when you visit a website or app. Cookies allow the identification of your device and collect information about the pages and features you interact with.

Cookies are either first-party cookies, placed and used by us (SiteHub), or third-party cookies, placed and used by partners such as analytics and advertising providers.##### **Cookies on our websites**

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to enable website functionality, remember preferences, analyze traffic, and personalize content. We use both first-party and third-party cookies, including session and persistent cookies.##### **Types of cookies we use**

**Necessary**                                         help make a website usable by enabling basic functions such as page navigation and access to secure areas. These cookies do not require consent.

**Functional**remember choices you make, such as language or region, to personalize your experience.  Currently, there are functional cookies in use.

**Analystics/Statistical**help us understand how visitors interact with our websites. All data is collected anonymously.

**Marketing**track users across websites to display relevant ads and measure campaign effectiveness. These are only set if you give consent.

**Unclassified**are cookies that are in the process of being classified together with the providers##### **Legal basis for using cookies**

Our use of cookies is based on the following legal grounds:

Necessary cookies: Legitimate interest (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f))

Other cookies: Only set with your explicit consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a))

We comply with the the General Data Protection Regulation (Regulation (EU) 2016/679) and the Data Protection Act (the “ Data Protection Laws”).##### **Consent and withdrawal**

You can manage your cookie settings at any time by clicking the cookie settings button on the bottom right.Previously accepted cookies can be deleted via your browser settings.##### **Personal data and third parties**

If you consent to non-essential cookies, we may process personal data such as: IP address, browser and device type, website interactions.

Some of this data may be shared with trusted third parties (e.g. analytics and marketing providers) for the stated purposes.##### **Cookie declaration & question about cookies?**

We use recognized, industry-standard cookie platforms to manage and store cookies, including those based on IP address and browsing behaviour.

For transparency and relevance, we do not publish a detailed list of cookies here. If you have questions about specific cookies, their purpose, or data processing, you are welcome to contact us: **[dpo@sitehub.dk](mailto:dpo@sitehub.dk)**.
